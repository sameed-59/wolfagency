Why are advertisements important?


Advertisements are a guaranteed method of reaching an audience. By creating an engaging ad, and spending enough to reach your target users, advertisements can have an immediate impact on business. This effect could be seen in improved trade or boosted brand recognition, among many different metrics advertising can take many forms, including text, banners, push notifications and pre or post-roll video ads.
